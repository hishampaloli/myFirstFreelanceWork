Course Offered

The Hidayathul Islam Da’wa College offers five year secondary program of the Hudawi Course. It offers a well-planned and scientifically designed five year course which is the basic and primary arena in the 12 year academic program of the Darul Huda Islamic University. Highly intelligent and brilliant students aged between 10-11.5 are admitted to the Secondary level following rigorous written and oral admission tests. Darul Huda Islamic University designed this program to give students the necessary basis of knowledge of language, and Islamic and Contemporary disciplines that enables students tread quietly and cautiously the path ahead of academic program. Secondary also provide an educational environment within which students acquire skill necessary for the realization of their potential. Syllabus consists of books that give primary lessons of five languages, namely, Arabic, English, Urdu, Hindi and Malayalam and other disciplines that belong to both from religious and contemporary.
