Services Overview

E-learning

It facilitates the technology enhanced with audio-visual facilities, that helps in teaching and learning process. Audio-visual equipments facilitate video capturing of the online classes especially during the Covid-19 pandemic.

Library

In the fond memory of former Chief Minister CH Muhammed Koya, the college library is named “CH Memorial Library”. The library contains good number of books, magazines, newspapers and other resources in various disciplines.

Masjid

Campus is having a Masjid, to offer prayer for students, teachers and staff. Masjid provides opportunity for the students to practice the islamic learning. Students practice Prayers,Duas, Moulid, Swalath, Majlisunnoor etc apart from practicing islamic speeches under the supervision of teachers.

Auditorium

The new auditorium, facilitates to organise various programmes like workshop, seminar, and cultural events of students.

Hostel

The Hidayathul Islam Da’wa College is a fully residential campus, and all students and faculty members residing in the campus and are provided with free hostel facility inside the campus.

Sports Facilities

College provides ample scope for sports and physical exercise for the students. Students use play-ground for football, cricket, badminton and other sports items.

About Us

Hidayathul Islam Da’wa College , one of the prestigious Islamic initiatives of southern Kerala, located in shore of Arabian sea at Kovalam which affiliated to Darul Huda Islamic University.
