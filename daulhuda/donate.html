Donate Us

Hidayathul Islam Da’wa College, is a fully a residential campus. The education, food and accommodation are completely free for all students admitted to this college. The college is run by the financial support from the people of all walks who love the college and are interested in supporting this institution. Since the college incurs a huge amount for its day to day expenditure we request all kind hearted people to generously support the college financially or by way of contributing food, grocery/vegetables & fruits/any other items useful for the college. You may also sponsor special food during birthday/marriage/anniversary or similar special occasions and be part of the special prayers offered by the students and teachers of the college. Those interested in sponsoring complete expense of a student may also do so.

Special Dua (Prayers) will be done for those who contribute for this noble cause during weekly swalath held on Thursdays after Magrib prayer.
