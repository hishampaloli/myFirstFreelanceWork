About Us

Hidayathul Islam Da’wa College, one of the prestigious Islamic initiatives of southern Kerala, located in the shore of Arabian sea at Kovalam in Thiruvananthapuram, India is affiliated to the Darul Huda Islamic University, Chemmad, Malappuram, Kerala. It was established in 2009 under the stewardship of Kovalam Muslim Jama’th which takes care educational initiatives including Islamic Madrassa and other religious activities. The college is ultimately focused at moulding a well equipped generation who are resourceful with both Islamic and secular studies. The College offers its flagship programme namely, Hudawi course combining moral education and school education.

Principal's Message

Hidyathul Islam Da’wa College (HIDC) is an Islamic institution which is situated in Thruvanathapuram, at Kovalam. It was established in 2009 as one of the branches of DARUL HUDA ISLAMIC UNIVERSITY (DHIU), based at Malappuram in the state of Kerala. HIDC as the member of DHIU, follows the syllabus provided by DHIU, completely applying the mission and vision of Islamic atmosphere in the southern part of Kerala. Our mission is to develop an Islamic educational framework in order to prepare religious scientists (Ulama) who acquire, practice, and propagate Islam, being alert of their ultimate responsibilities towards Almighty Allah and towards their own family, institution, society, surroundings and private and public resources and to make them capable of competing the shifting trends of modern education. To accomplish this mission, we provide material and religious education for the students fostering their linguistic ability by including five important different languages; Arabic, English, Urdu, Hindi and Malayalam. It allows them to propagate Islam in various parts of the world.
-Muhammed Aslam Hudawi, Principal
